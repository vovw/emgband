$$
X_k = \sum_{n=0}^{N-1} x_n e^{-i2\pi kn /N}
$$
$$x_{even} = (x_0,x_2,x_4,...,x_{N-2})$$
$$ x_{odd} = (x_1,x_3,x_5,...x_{N-1})$$
$$ X_k = X_{even,k} + e^{-i2\pi k /N}X_(odd,k)$$
$$X_{k+N/2} = X_{even,k} - e^{-i2\pi k/N}X_{odd,k}$$
